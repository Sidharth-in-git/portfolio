Car Price Analysis & Visualization

Exploratory analysis, model comparison, and interactive visualizations of used car pricing data.

Overview

This project performs exploratory data analysis and visualization to understand used car price drivers. The workflow includes cleaning, categorical encoding, feature engineering (age, mileage adjustments), model training for comparison (linear models, RandomForest, XGBoost), and rich visualizations to compare models and feature effects.

Key points:

  • Careful feature engineering to capture non-linear effects (age, mileage buckets).
  • Model evaluation and comparison (MAE, hold-out validation) with visual performance summaries.
  • Result: comparative analysis and interactive visualizations that highlight model strengths and areas for improvement.

Tech: Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, XGBoost
