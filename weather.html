Weather Forecasting App

7-day forecasting with uncertainty visualizations

Overview

This project uses historical weather data to produce short-term forecasts (temperature and precipitation). It explores models like Prophet and ARIMA, implements time-aware cross-validation, and provides a small FastAPI endpoint to serve predictions and a Streamlit front-end for visualization.

Key points:

  • Focus on seasonality, holidays, and recent trends in feature engineering.
  • Produces prediction intervals to communicate uncertainty.
  • Result: prototype forecasts and examples of uncertainty visualization.

Tech: Python, Prophet/ARIMA, Pandas, FastAPI, Streamlit
